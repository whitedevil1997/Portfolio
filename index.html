About Me

Hi, I'm Dennis, a concept artist/ illustrator. Rather than to say I'm a self-taught artist, I grew up learning from other great artists. Always being keen on the difference between seeing and observing, I would prefer to observe things. My flexibility ranges from a long shot matte painting to a clear defined Character designing.

A tireless learner and always open to receive productive critiques. Most of the time, use to provide driving and motivational thoughts for the needy. In parallel, I'm self-motivating, and tracking my self-improvisation over time is a key.
